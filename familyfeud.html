🎤 MC Instructions

1. The Setup: Split guests into Team Lyn and Team Jamilah.

2. The Face-Off: One person from each team comes up. First to buzz (squeaky toy) gets to guess. If they get a board answer, their team chooses to PLAY or PASS.

3. Playing the Board: The team takes turns guessing. 3 strikes and the other team gets ONE chance to steal.

4. Hints: If everyone is stuck, click "GIVE HINT" to show the first letter of all hidden answers!

5. Birthday Rules: If you get a strike, take a sip. If your team loses a round, take a gulp!
